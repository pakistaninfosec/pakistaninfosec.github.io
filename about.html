About InfoSec Pakistan
Pakistan's First
Automated Threat
Intelligence Platform
InfoSec Pakistan is a free, automated cybersecurity threat intelligence platform built specifically for Pakistani organizations, security professionals, and government agencies. We aggregate, analyze, and deliver daily threat digests so Pakistan stays ahead of cyber threats.
Our Mission
To democratize cybersecurity intelligence in Pakistan by providing free, automated, AI-powered threat intelligence that helps organizations protect their digital assets and respond to emerging threats before they cause damage.

Our Journey

🚀 Project Started
InfoSec Pakistan scraper built on Replit with initial sources — NVD, CISA KEV and basic threat aggregation.

🤖 Groq AI Integration
Added Groq-powered LLaMA AI analysis for automatic executive summary generation in daily email digests.

Launched daily HTML email digest with color-coded severity badges — Zero Day (red), Actively Exploited (orange), Pakistan Alert (green).
